About BantuPay

Empowering financial freedom, for everyone.

A mobile banking platform built to move money instantly, securely, and without friction — for families, businesses, and communities in the D.R. Congo and beyond.

Our story

It started with a frustration many people know too well. Sending money to loved ones in the D.R. Congo is never simple — it's slow, expensive, and unpredictable.

For families, students, and entrepreneurs, this wasn't just inconvenient. It was a real barrier. We believed it shouldn't be this way. So we built BantuPay.

A simple idea: what if sending money felt as easy as sending a message? What if anyone, in Kinshasa or abroad, could move money instantly, securely, and without friction?

Our mission

Financial freedom, no matter where you are.

For too long, access to financial services has depended on where you live or who you know. We believe that must change.

Our mission is to replace those barriers with simple, powerful tools that just work — send, receive, and use money instantly.

For millions, something as basic as getting paid is still a challenge. Opportunities are missed. Businesses grow slower.

BantuPay exists to make financial freedom accessible to everyone. Because access to money isn't just convenience — it's independence.
